Philippine Music Genres

Pinoy POP

Pinoy pop, also known as Philippine pop or Pilipino pop,
refers to a contemporary pop music in the Philippines originating from the OPM genre.
With its beginnings in the late 1970s, Pinoy pop is a growing genre in year of the 2020s.

RnB

Rhythm and blues, frequently abbreviated as R&B or R'n'B,
is a genre of popular music that originated within African-American communities in the 1940s.
