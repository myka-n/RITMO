OPM History Evolution

Original Pilipino Music, commonly known as OPM, is a term used to refer to music created by Filipino artists in the Philippines or abroad. OPM has a long and rich history that spans over several decades, and it has played a significant role in shaping Filipino culture and identity. In this essay, we will explore the origins of OPM, its evolution over the years, and its impact on Filipino society.
